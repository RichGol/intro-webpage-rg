Greatest Computing Innovations

  • I consider wearable technology to be a great computing innovation. It is a testament to the continued improvement of computing power in small form factors with practical applications (like health and safety). I'm not thrilled about the privacy issues created by wearable technology, but it is innovative in both technology and function.

  • Machine Learning (sometimes called Artificial Intelligence) is another computing innovation that I consider to be important. It is a great innovation because of the field of study it has created, and for the applications it has been used in.

  • The Internet is perhaps one of the greatest innovations in computing. ARPANet's use of standardized protocols to send data over a network of interconnected machines laid the foundation for nearly all computing applications today.
